Frequently Asked Questions

What if no data is found for my VIN? +
If CarHaki cannot find any records for the VIN you entered, we will refund your payment in full within 24 hours. No questions asked.
How quickly do I receive my report? +
Most reports are generated instantly — within 30 seconds of payment. For unusual VINs it may take up to 5 minutes. You will receive an email notification when your report is ready.
Can I share the report with others? +
Yes. Every report has a unique shareable link that works for anyone — even without a CarHaki account. Perfect for sharing with family, mechanics, or insurance brokers.
Where does the data come from? +
CarHaki pulls data from NMVTIS (US National Motor Vehicle Title Information System), NHTSA safety recall database, state DMV title records, and US insurance databases.
Does CarHaki work for all cars sold in Nigeria? +
CarHaki currently supports US-sourced vehicles (Tokunbo cars imported from the United States) using the 17-character VIN number. These account for the majority of Tokunbo vehicles entering Nigeria through Cotonou, Apapa, and Tin Can Island ports.
