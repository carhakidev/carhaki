Tips for finding the number

For USA vehicles:

  • - Look through the windscreen at the dashboard on the driver's side
  • - Check inside the driver's door on the door frame sticker
  • - Find it on the vehicle registration or insurance documents
  • - 17 characters, no I, O, or Q

For Japan vehicles:

  • - Check the firewall (panel between engine and cabin)
  • - Look at the Japanese export certificate
  • - Auction sheets often show the chassis number at the top
  • - Usually in format MODEL-XXXXXX (e.g. ZZT240-987654)
